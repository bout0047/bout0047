How to apply to hHZ university of applied science?

Follow the two steps explained below to apply for one of our Bachelor (Associate Degree) programmes. We accept applications beginning in October which means you can apply before finishing your secondary education. We advise you to apply as soon as possible so that you have enough time to prepare for your study. To study at HZ, you must first apply online via Studielink, the online application system for higher education in the Netherlands. Follow the instructions carefully because you will need to follow the whole procedure for your application request to be complete.
